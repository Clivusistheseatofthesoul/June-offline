## Aetiology
### Normal Mg2+ homeostasis
Serum Mg correlate very poorly with total body Mg
- 50% in the bones
- 50% in intracellular fluid
- Of the 1% remaining:
	- 20% bound to albumin
	- 10% complexed with anions
	- 70% unbound and biologically active
