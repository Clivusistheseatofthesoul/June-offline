# [[Skin desquamation]] specifically starting from underneath the finger nails
## Description:
### Desquamation which starts under the nails -> spreads up the fingers and into the hands
### This is a **late sign**
## DDx:
### [[Kawasaki disease]]
## Mimics:
### Mechanic's hands
### S-J syndrome can look similar
